I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Southern California, very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jiapeng Zhang.

I am broadly interested in simple yet profound theories and general yet efficient techniques. During my PhD, my research focused primarily on communication complexity. More recently, I am also interested in provable quantum advantages.

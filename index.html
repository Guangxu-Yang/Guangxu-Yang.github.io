Guangxu Yang 杨广绪

I am a Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Jiapeng Zhang at University of Southern California.

My research focuses on communication complexity. Recently, my work has centered on two main themes:

  • Developing new lifting techniques for proving lower bounds in the Number-on-Forehead (NOF) communication model.
  • Advancing the gadgetless lifting framework, especially for analyzing round–communication trade-offs.

These two themes have broad applications in circuit complexity, cryptography, and quantum computing.
